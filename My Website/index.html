High Frequency Signal Simulation Undergraduate Research
MATLAB ¬∑ Electromagnetic Computation ¬∑ FDTF

Working for University of Colorado Boulder's High Frequency Signal Simulation lab, I perform MATLAB simulations using Finite Difference Time Domain (FDTD) method. FDTD uses a discrete form of Maxwell's equations to simulate electromagnetic behavior. My role is to conduct experiments that help expose and quantify the limitations of this idustry used computational method

  • Simulated high frequency surface waves above planar circuits.
  • Contributing to ongoing research with regards to absorbing boundary conditions (ABC's) in FDTD.
  • Documented project extensively, including version control, and data management.
